Topic I: Understanding Global Warming

Global Warming is one of the most critical issue facing humanity today. As our planet's climate continues to change at an alarming rate, it is important for people to take action to prevent its impacts. Global Warming is the long-term increase in Earth's average surface temperature. This primarily occur due to human activities that release greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide. Our website will provide you, reader, crucial information about global warming, its causes, effects, and possible solutions.



Topic II: Cauases of Global Warming

The list below are some of the main contributors to Global Warming.


  • The burning of fuels such as coal for energy production releases carbon dioxide into the atmosphere.
  • Deforestation refers to the clearing of trees for some purposes which reduces the amount of trees that absorbs the carbon dioxide from the atmosphere.
  • Industrial processes such as metal smelting, and chemical manufacturing releases greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide.
  • Poor or Improper Waste Management releases greenhouse gases such as methane and carbon dioxide to our atmosphere.
  • Forest fires release large amounts of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.


Topic III: Effects of Global Warming

The list below are some possible effects of Global Warming.


  • Melting of glaciers contribute to higher sea levels, posing a threat to communities and ecosystems.
  • Increase on temperatures lead to intense heatwaves, storms, and floods, causing damage and loss of lives.
  • Shifts in temperature disrupt ecosystems, endangering living organisms.
  • Climate change results to human health problems such as heat-related illnesses, respiratory diseases, and spread of infectious diseases.
  • Changes in temperature affects agricultural and food productivity.


Topic IV: Possible Solutions to Global Warming

The lsit below are some possible solutions to Global Warming.


  • Use energy more efficiently and choose renewabale energy sources.
  • Support policies that promotes renewabale energy.
  • Educate others about the importance of preventing global warming.
